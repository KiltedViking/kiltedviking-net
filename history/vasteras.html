Anund's Cairn and Badelunda

East of Västerås is one of Sweden's larges burial cairns, Anund's Cairn (Anundshögen), and burial ship formation.

In Badelunda there is a stone maze (more of a round pattern, in the form of a maze, built with small round stones, almost Celtic looking).
